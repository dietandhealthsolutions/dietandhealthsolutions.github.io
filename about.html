About Diet and Health Solutions

Founded by Prof. Daniel McCartney, Lecturer in Human Nutrition & Dietetics, award-winning software engineer Stephen Farrell and Medical Consultant Dr. Declan Byrne, we provide evidence-based dietary advice tools to manage a range of clinical conditions. We also provide state of the art training courses in nutrition, diet and health for the general public and for clinicians seeking to up-skill in an area whose role in health and disease is increasingly recognised.

So if you're interested in improving your health through diet, or if you're a clinician or professional seeking evidence-based education in the area of nutrition, or even if you're just interested in finding out more about diet, nutrition and health from a personal point of view; you've come to the right place.

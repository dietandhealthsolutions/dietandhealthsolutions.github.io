About Diet and Health Solutions

Founded by Prof. Daniel McCartney, Lecturer in Human Nutrition & Dietetics, award-winning software engineer Stephen Farrell and Medical Consultant Dr. Declan Byrne, we provide evidence-based dietary advice tools to manage a range of clinical conditions. We also provide state of the art training courses in nutrition, diet and health for the general public and for clinicians seeking to up-skill in an area whose role in health and disease is increasingly recognised.

So if you're interested in improving your health through diet, or if you're a clinician or professional seeking evidence-based education in the area of nutrition, or even if you're just interested in finding out more about diet, nutrition and health from a personal point of view; you've come to the right place.

Prof. Daniel McCartney

Managing Director

Prof. Daniel McCartney holds a B.Sc. in Human Nutrition & Dietetics, a M.Sc. in Molecular Medicine and a Ph.D. in Public Health and Nutritional Epidemiology. He has been a Lecturer in Human Nutrition & Dietetics at DIT and Adjunct Asst. Professor of Dietetics at Trinity College Dublin since 2003; and is a co-founder of Diet and Health Solutions Ltd. He specialises in the dietary treatment and prevention of chronic disease (e.g. obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, cancer, osteoporosis), with a special interest in the diet and nutritional health of young women, and in the fetal programming of disease. Since 2012, Dan’s research work has focussed on maternal diet, lifestyle and bodyweight and their effects on pregnancy outcome, particularly fetal health. He is widely published in the scientific and medical literature in these areas.

Dan is currently supervising three post-graduate students in the area of maternal and infant nutrition. He is also a participant on the Irish Nutrition & Dietetic Institute's (INDI) inaugural Management and Leadership Programme at the Irish Management Institute (IMI), and is a former Public Relations Officer for INDI. He has been a fortnightly columnist for the Irish Examiner since May 2012.

Stephen Farrell

Technical Director

Stephen Farrell holds a 1st Class (hons.) B.Sc. in Software Systems from NCI and is a co–founder of Diet and Health Solutions Ltd. He has specialist experience in web-application architecture and development, including Java (Spring), PHP (Symfony, Zend Framework), JavaScript (AngularJS, jQuery), HTML5, CSS, Web Services (REST, SOAP). He has almost 10 years experience leading the design and implementation of various IT systems from internal business information systems and banking software to high volume websites and social apps/games for the gaming industry, and has worked for several prominent IT firms in Dublin including Version 1 and Pocket Kings. Stephen is responsible for the design and delivery of our web based software platform and brings his experience in both technical architecture and product design to constantly refine and improve our software offerings. He is currently a Senior Enterprise Architect at Workforce-Velocity.

Dr. Declan Byrne

Medical Director

Dr. Declan Byrne is a specialist in general, geriatric and stroke medicine. In addition he holds specialist qualifications in clinical nutrition and pharmaceutical medicine. He practices as a consultant physician at St. James's Hospital, Dublin and he is a Senior Lecturer in Medical Gerontology at Trinity College, Dublin. Declan has also been elected as Chairman of the board of the Irish Nutrition and Dietetic Institute (INDI), and is a Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians of Ireland (RCPI) where he holds positions as regional specialty adviser for general internal medicine, member of the MRCPI clinical exams committee and member of the Continuous Medical Education (CME) and professional development committees.
